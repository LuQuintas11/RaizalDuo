Raizal is an Argentine folk music duo made up of Agus Mercado on vocals , and Facu Quintas on piano. The project was generated within the framework of the Folklore class at the Faculty of Arts of the National University of Cordoba, Argentina in 2019. Since then they have participated in different festivals and concerts in different cities like Cordoba Capitals, Cosquin, Villa Maria and La Rioja ..

MUSIC

On July 1, 2022, our first EP was released on all digital platforms, which is made up of 4 songs: two Vidala, a Zamba of our own authorship, and a Chaya by the Riojan composer Carlos Paredes. The musical production was in charge of Juan Murúa, recorded at Estudio Liverpool by Alfredo Guerra, mixed and mastered at Mate Místico by Matías Pérez. The release by Guido Gironelli through the Elephant in the Room record label. If you want to know more about our music and culture check our Friends
